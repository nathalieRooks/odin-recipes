Darth vader cookies

ingredients

  • 230 gram semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 Darth Vader silicone mold
  • Sugar for rolling
  • 300-500 gram all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 170 gram unsalted butter
  • 225 gram peanut butter
  • 42 gram sugar
  • 42 gram brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

explanation.

  1. Place the chocolate chips in a heat-safe bowl and microwave at 15-20 second intervals until melted. Spoon evenly into the silicone mold, tapping to release excess air bubbles. Place in the freezer to set.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius. Prep baking sheets with parchment or silpats.
  3. In a bowl whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  4. In the bowl of an electric mixer cream the butter, peanut butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  5. Add the egg, and vanilla.
  6. Add the dry ingredients until the dough just comes together.
  7. Shape the dough into 2-inch balls, then roll in sugar and place on the prepped baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 10 minutes.
  9. Remove the chocolate from the molds and press into the center of each cookie.
  10. Transfer to a wire rack to let cool completely.

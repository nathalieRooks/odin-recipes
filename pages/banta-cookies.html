Bantha cookies

Bantha cookies

ingredients

  • 115g butter, softened
  • 75g granulated sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 240g plain flour

explanation.

  1. Beat the butter, sugar, salt and vanilla together until soft anf fluffy. Mix in the egg yolk. Add the flour and mix well.Spoon onto a sheet of cling film and shape into a 20 x 5cm log. Wrap well and chill for 30 mins.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200C/180C Fan/Gas 6 and line 2 baking sheets with parchment.
  3. Cut the cookie log into 16-18 slices and arrange on lined baking sheets well spaced apart. Cook for 10-12 mins until just starting to go golden round the edges. Lift onto a cooling rack and leave to cool.
